# Marketing Guide

## Positioning Statement

Kairis is a disciplined execution system for crypto traders who want clearer decisions, stronger controls, and safer paths from signal to action.

## Messaging Pillars

### 1. Control Before Execution

Kairis helps users decide when to act and when not to act.

### 2. Clear Modes, Clear Authority

Users can operate in manual, assisted, or qualified automated workflows with visible boundaries.

### 3. Trust Through Records

Signals, approvals, orders, and outcomes are logged so users can understand what happened.

### 4. Safety For Real Users

The product is built for people who need structure, not hype.

## Tone Guidelines

- plainspoken
- calm
- technically competent
- never breathless

## Claims To Avoid

- guaranteed returns
- easy money
- set and forget
- AI beats the market
- retirement through crypto automation

## Recommended Language

- disciplined execution
- safer trading workflows
- visible controls
- structured decision support
- non-custodial trading software

## Target Audience Narrative

For beginners:

- "Start with paper trading, then graduate into live execution with guardrails."

For more active users:

- "Keep your exchange, keep your custody, improve your operating discipline."

## Launch Content Themes

- why most beginners fail from execution mistakes, not lack of information
- why mode-based trading is safer than jumping straight to bots
- how to connect exchange APIs safely
- how to think about limits, journaling, and halt states

## Brand Expression

The visual and verbal system should communicate:

- calm control
- timing with discipline
- seriousness without institutional stiffness
