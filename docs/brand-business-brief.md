# Brand + Business Brief

## Overview

Kairis is a non-custodial crypto trading system designed around disciplined execution. The product gives users a structured way to move between manual, assisted, and automated trading modes without surrendering custody or relying on hype-driven claims.

The core business is not "sell alpha." The business is to sell safer trading operations software for active users who want clearer controls, better logs, and stronger boundaries between decision support and order execution.

## Brand Foundation

- Working name: `Kairis`
- Pronunciation: `KAI-riss`
- Root meaning: derived from `kairos`, the right or opportune moment
- Brand interpretation: not speed for its own sake, but the discipline to act at the right time under defined limits
- Selected logo direction: `Threshold K`

## Brand Positioning

Kairis should read as a serious operating layer for traders, not a meme-coin app, not a broker, and not an "AI makes money while you sleep" product.

The brand should emphasize:

- control over impulse
- clear thresholds for when automation is allowed
- visible risk boundaries
- reliable execution records
- confidence through clarity, not promises

## Visual Identity Direction

Selected logo concept:

- `Threshold K`
- reference asset: `assets/logos/kairis-threshold-k.svg`

Meaning:

- the `K` acts as a branded threshold marker
- the interrupted/gated structure represents controlled transition from signal to execution
- the visual system should feel closer to an instrument panel than a retail finance app

Primary visual cues:

- deep graphite background
- muted green signal accent
- restrained brass highlight
- sharp geometry with controlled asymmetry

## Product Identity

Kairis is best framed as:

- a disciplined execution system
- a trader operating layer
- a control surface for signals, approvals, and automated spot execution

Kairis is not framed as:

- a brokerage
- an exchange
- a custodian
- a guaranteed-return trading bot
- a copy-trading network

## Customer Framing

Primary launch customer:

- beginner retail crypto users who want help acting on signals without managing everything manually

Secondary customer:

- more advanced traders who value logs, limits, repeatability, and mode switching

Internal founder use:

- full automation with stricter controls and owner-only capabilities

## Core Product Thesis

Most retail trading tools either do too little or too much. Dashboards stop at visibility. Trading bots jump straight to automation. Kairis fills the gap by giving users staged control:

- manual mode for observation and direct action
- assisted mode for guided execution with explicit user confirmation
- automated mode for qualified accounts under hard guardrails

## Business Model

Kairis is sold as software, not as brokerage infrastructure.

Revenue model:

- free tier for signals, education, paper mode, and limited analytics
- paid tier for connected exchange workflows, advanced journaling, exports, controls, and assisted execution
- internal-only auto mode initially
- optional future premium automation tier only after controls and support burden are proven

## Trust Position

Trust is a product feature, not a marketing layer.

Kairis must earn trust through:

- transparent execution modes
- explicit API permission guidance
- audit logs for signals and orders
- visible risk controls
- simple language about product limits

## Domain and Naming Notes

The brand does not require a `.com` in the validation phase. Preferred domain patterns:

- `kairis.io`
- `getkairis.com`
- `kairisapp.com`
- `kairis.trade`

Repo naming should remain aligned with the product name even if the public domain uses a prefix.

## Strategic Constraints

- no custody
- no pooled user funds
- no leverage or derivatives in v1
- no withdrawal permissions on exchange API keys
- no profit guarantees or hype-driven marketing

## Success Criteria For The Brand

- users immediately understand the product is about control, not gambling
- the name can survive expansion beyond crypto-only tooling
- messaging stays compatible with a more serious compliance posture later
